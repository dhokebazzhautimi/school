OUR PROGRAMS

CURRICULUM

Academic Departments

Our schooling frameworks include the following service areas.

Early Child Development

Early Child Development(ECD) at GPHS comprises of Child Care, Play group, Nursery, Lower KG and Upper KG classes.

Basic Level Education

Basic Level Education includes the education that is imparted to the students of grade 1 to 8.

Secondary Level Education

The education imparted to the students of grade 9 and 10 is secondary education. At GPS, government syllabus is strictly followed along with some extra subject materials to develop the personality and enhance knowledge and skill of the students.

Extra Curricular Programs

We believe that the students active in creative arts, like music, drama and visual arts tend to be open-minded, eccentric and think outside of the box

Young Musicians

Our students can learn to read music notation and symbols, play instruments, while fostering their ability to grasp knowledge and inter-personnel skills.

Art & Design

We believe that the students active in creative arts, like music, drama and visual arts tend to be open-minded, eccentric and think outside of the box.

Math & Science Clubs

We believe in bringing best out of students by providing opportunities to join clubs where they can ping their ideas and work in teams to polish their creative sides.

Competitions

We timely organise sport competitions, quiz competitions , debates programmes ,etc. which aids in building growth mindset and self-confidence in the students.

Afterschool Sports

Sports being a key factor in maintaining fitness levels, confidence, resilience in the students, we provide students with quality sporting facilities alongside good trainers .
