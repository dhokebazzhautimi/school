Welcome to Holy Garden English Secondary School (HGESS)

Holy Garden English Secondary School is an English medium school established in 1991 A.D. It is located at a very convenient place at Saraswatinagar, Chabahil, Kathmandu. The school has stimulating, vibrant and genial environment. It is free from harmful dust and sound pollution. There is enough space/ground for extra co-curricular activities. This institution is established with sole iron determination for imparting quality education to the students. Holy Garden prides in being one of the top schools in Kathmandu. We provide Life-wide Quality Education with excellence to create a new generation of leaders and professionals with a caring and responsible attitude to society, inside and outside the typical spectrum.

OBJECTIVES

  • Provide a safe and nurturing environment for students to learn and grow.
  • Deliver high-quality education that meets academic standards and prepares students for future success.
  • Promote critical thinking, problem-solving, and creativity among students.
  • Foster a positive and inclusive school culture that values diversity and promotes respect for others.
  • Encourage active student engagement in the learning process through interactive teaching methods.
  • Support the social and emotional development of students, promoting their well-being and mental health.
  • Cultivate strong partnerships between parents, teachers, and the community to enhance student learning and achievement.
  • Provide opportunities for extracurricular activities and enrichment programs to develop students' interests and talents.
  • Implement effective assessment and feedback systems to monitor student progress and guide instructional planning.
  • Promote digital literacy and integrate technology in the learning process to enhance educational outcomes.
